# AUNO Center institutional introduction — production brief

## Master format

- Duration: 60 seconds
- Primary frame: 16:9, 4K or 1080p
- Social adaptations: 30 seconds in 1:1 and 9:16; 15 seconds in 9:16
- Language: English
- Voice: calm, clear, confident, globally intelligible
- Music: subtle instrumental only, licensed for institutional and social use
- Accessibility: burned-in captions for social exports, separate WebVTT captions, transcript, high-contrast text, no rapid flashing

## 60-second storyboard

1. **0–4 seconds — Identity**  
   Visual: AUNO crest emerges over the global network motif.  
   On-screen: “One World. One Research. One Center of Excellence.”

2. **4–19 seconds — Connected challenges**  
   Visual: permission-cleared research, healthcare, animal wellbeing, earth, nature, and education imagery.  
   Voiceover: first two transcript paragraphs.

3. **19–29 seconds — Institutional role**  
   Visual: interdisciplinary discussion, research planning, evidence review, learning.  
   On-screen: “Research support · Collaboration · Innovation · Training”

4. **29–40 seconds — Who AUNO serves**  
   Visual: students, researchers, healthcare professionals, and institutional teams.  
   On-screen: “Question → Design → Evidence → Meaning”

5. **40–49 seconds — Values**  
   Visual: consent, secure data practice, transparent analysis, peer discussion.  
   On-screen: “Ethics · Transparency · Context · Capability”

6. **49–60 seconds — Call to action**  
   Visual: crest, website, and official social identity.  
   On-screen: “Research with clarity. Knowledge with purpose.” and “aunocenter.netlify.app · @aunocenter”

## 30-second social cut

One world. One research. One center of excellence. AUNO Center connects human health, animal wellbeing, earth, nature, education, and responsible innovation through multidisciplinary research support, collaboration, training, and knowledge exchange. We help researchers move from an important question to rigorous methods and clear scientific communication. AUNO Center—research with clarity, knowledge with purpose.

## 15-second social cut

Research challenges cross disciplines and borders. AUNO Center connects research support, education, collaboration, and responsible innovation for human health, animal wellbeing, earth, and nature. One world. One research. One center of excellence.

## Publication checklist

- Confirm written permission for identifiable people and locations.
- Confirm licenses for footage, images, fonts, voice, and music.
- Verify every institutional and research claim.
- Include captions and publish the transcript.
- Preserve legible safe margins in 16:9, 1:1, and 9:16 crops.
- Include the consultancy disclaimer in the description.
- Do not imply accreditation, partnerships, completed projects, or guaranteed outcomes without evidence.
