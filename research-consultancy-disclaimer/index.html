Professional boundary

Research Consultancy Disclaimer

AUNO Center provides educational, methodological, analytical, editorial, and capacity-building support. Public website content and initial communications are general information, not project-specific professional advice.

A26

No guaranteed outcome

AUNO Center does not guarantee publication, funding, academic grades, ethics approval, regulatory approval, institutional acceptance, statistically significant findings, or any other outcome controlled by third parties or dependent on the underlying research.

Researcher responsibility

Support does not transfer accountability.

Researchers and institutions remain responsible for their source information, decisions, approvals, use of outputs, final work, and communication.

Core responsibilities

The requester remains responsible for

  1. 01

    Accuracy, legality, provenance, and permitted use of data and materials

  2. 02

    Ethics, privacy, institutional, funder, publisher, professional, and regulatory requirements

  3. 03

    Final methodological, statistical, clinical, and professional decisions

  4. 04

    Verification of calculations, citations, outputs, interpretations, and conclusions

  5. 05

    Appropriate disclosure of consultancy, editorial, statistical, translation, and AI assistance

  6. 06

    Authorship, contribution, acknowledgement, and conflict decisions

  7. 07

    Secure handling of confidential, identifiable, proprietary, and otherwise sensitive information

Research integrity

AUNO Center does not support plagiarism, fabricated or falsified data, invented citations, undisclosed ghost authorship, misleading statistical practices, manipulation or concealment of results, circumvention of academic rules, or bypassing ethics and institutional requirements.

Statistical and methodological limits

Results depend on study design, data quality, measurement, sampling, assumptions, missingness, analytical choices, uncertainty, and context. No method can guarantee a desired finding or repair data that cannot credibly answer the question.

Healthcare and clinical boundary

Healthcare-related support is for research purposes. It is not diagnosis, treatment, medical advice, emergency assistance, legal advice, privacy advice, regulatory approval, or ethics approval. It must not replace qualified clinical or institutional judgement.

Public resources

Articles, checklists, website pages, and initial communications are general educational information. They are not tailored to a project or jurisdiction and should not be relied on as a substitute for authorised advice.

Engagement terms

Any confidential engagement, deliverable, fee, timeline, secure data-transfer process, intellectual-property arrangement, or allocation of responsibility must be governed by a separate agreement or clearly confirmed terms.

Before requesting support

  • Use non-confidential information in the initial form.
  • Identify required approvals and institutional rules.
  • Be transparent about project stage and deadlines.
  • Retain responsibility for verification and final use.
