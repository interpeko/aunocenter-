# Statistical Analysis Plan Checklist

Complete before inspecting outcome patterns whenever possible.

- Research questions, objectives, and hypotheses are explicit.
- Population, inclusion rules, exclusions, and analysis sets are defined.
- Primary and secondary outcomes are operationally defined.
- Exposures, predictors, covariates, confounders, and effect modifiers are defined.
- Variable coding, units, transformations, derived variables, and time points are documented.
- Descriptive summaries and visualisations are specified.
- Primary effect measure or estimand is stated.
- Statistical methods match the design and data structure.
- Assumptions and diagnostic checks are stated.
- Missing-data assessment and handling are planned.
- Multiplicity, subgroup, sensitivity, and exploratory analyses are labelled.
- Sample-size or precision rationale is connected to the primary objective.
- Tables, figures, software, versioning, and reproducibility steps are planned.
- Interpretation will address magnitude, uncertainty, limitations, and practical meaning.
- Deviations from the plan will be dated and explained.

Educational checklist only. Project-specific statistical review may still be necessary.
