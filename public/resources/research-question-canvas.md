# Research Question Canvas

Use this canvas before selecting a design or analysis.

## 1. Problem and purpose

- What real problem or knowledge gap matters?
- Who is affected, and in what context?
- What decision, explanation, estimate, or understanding should the study support?

## 2. Focused question

Write the question in one sentence. Define the population or setting, central phenomenon/exposure/intervention, comparison if relevant, and outcome or concept of interest.

## 5. Alignment check

- Does the proposed design answer the exact question?
- Can each concept be measured or explored responsibly?
- Is the planned analysis possible with the expected data?
- What cannot be concluded from this approach?
