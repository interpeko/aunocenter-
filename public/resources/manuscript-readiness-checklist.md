# Manuscript Readiness Checklist

## Integrity and contribution

- Authorship and contributorship have been discussed transparently.
- Conflicts, funding, approvals, consent, registrations, and AI assistance are disclosed as required.
- Text, data, images, tables, and citations are original or correctly licensed and attributed.

## Reporting

- The title and abstract accurately represent the study.
- The introduction states the problem, evidence gap, and objective without overclaiming.
- Methods are sufficiently clear for appraisal and appropriate reproduction.
- Results follow the stated plan and include uncertainty and relevant denominators.
- Tables and figures are necessary, legible, consistent, and interpretable alone.
- The discussion distinguishes findings from interpretation and speculation.
- Limitations, bias, generalisability, and alternative explanations are addressed.
- The conclusion matches the design and evidence.
- The relevant reporting guideline has been checked.

## Submission

- Journal scope and author instructions have been reviewed.
- References, files, word limits, declarations, and supplementary materials are complete.
- Every author has reviewed and approved the final submission.

Educational checklist only. It does not guarantee acceptance or replace journal, institutional, ethics, legal, or professional requirements.
