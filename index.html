A connected research journey

Strong research begins with strong decisions.

Every project depends on a connected set of decisions: what to ask, how to investigate it, what evidence to collect, how to analyse it, and how to report it with integrity.

AUNO Center brings structure to those decisions through practical methodological support, capacity building, and clear communication. Our role is not to replace the researcher. It is to help researchers understand their options, document their reasoning, recognise limitations, and produce work that is transparent, defensible, and useful.

How we work

A responsible approach to research support.

Good consultancy strengthens the project and the researcher’s ability to understand, explain, and defend the decisions behind it.

R

Rigor before shortcuts

Recommendations begin with the question, design, data, context, and appropriate reporting standards.

C

Capability, not dependency

Support is designed to increase understanding and improve future methodological decision-making.

I

Integrity throughout

Authorship, consent, privacy, transparency, conflicts, and responsible reporting remain central.

L

Clarity without jargon

Complex ideas are explained accurately in language that enables informed action.

Engagement pathway

A clear path from request to support.

Every engagement begins with scope, boundaries, responsibilities, and expected outputs—not assumptions.

01 / Share

Tell us about your project

Submit a short, non-confidential overview of the question, stage, needs, and timeline.

02 / Scope

Define the right support

We review the request, assess fit, and clarify the most useful and responsible boundaries.

03 / Agree

Confirm an approach

Deliverables, responsibilities, timing, communication, and any fees are agreed before work begins.

04 / Collaborate

Work with transparency

Decisions are explained, progress is documented, and next steps stay practical and clear.
